Scrapz was created in 7 days as my final project @ RefactorU. I created it for parents and teachers as a resource for crafting with children. Scrapz is easy and fun to use.
It allows the user to search for fun crafts by the supplies they already have on hand! Simply choose supplies from the drop down lists in the Build-A-Form on the Home page, and hit submit! The results will be filtered according to your search criteria.

Scrapz is still a work in progress.
